Earthquake Since 1900 Above 7 Magnitude

Most earthquake occurs near tectonic plates fault lines. Since 1900, there has been 1347 earthquakes of magnitude 7 or greater and 5 earthquakes of magnitude 9 or greater.

1900 - 2016

Here is the link to learn what actions you can take to help yourself and your family to remain safe and healthy in the event of an earthquake: www.cdc.gov/disasters/earthquakes
