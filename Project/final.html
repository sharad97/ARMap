Earthquake Since 1900 - 2016 Above 7 Magnitude

Compare Earthquake Casualties to Other Natural Disaster

According to data, majority of earthquake occurs at tectonic fault lines. Since 1900, there has been 1347 earthquakes of magnitude 7 or greater and 5 earthquakes of magnitude 9 or greater.

Here is the link to learn what actions you can take to help yourself and your family to remain safe and healthy in the event of an earthquake: www.cdc.gov/disasters/earthquakes
