Earthquake Since 1900 Above 7 Magnitude

Earthquake is a natural occurring event which occurs often and has huge impact in our lives. Earthquake destroys thousands of lives and properties every year. In this interactive visualization, it will show where each earthquake has occurred since 1900 above 7 magnitude in richter scale. I am using scientific data gathered by United States Geological Survey.
